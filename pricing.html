FAQ

Frequently asked questions

What counts as a token?
A token is roughly 4 characters or ¾ of a word in English. 1,000 tokens ≈ 750 words ≈ 3–4 paragraphs. Both input (what you send) and output (the model's response) count toward usage. Images are tokenized based on resolution — typically 100–2,000 tokens depending on detail level requested.
How does prompt caching work?
Prompt caching lets you mark repeated sections (like a long system prompt or document) to be cached server-side. Cached tokens cost only 10% of normal input price — up to 90% savings for apps with stable context. Cache TTL is 5 minutes. Available on Pro and above plans.
Do unused tokens roll over?
Plan token allocations reset on your billing date and do not roll over. Pay-as-you-go usage beyond your quota is billed per-token with no expiration. We recommend setting up usage alerts in the Console to avoid unexpected charges.
Can I upgrade or downgrade my plan at any time?
Yes. Upgrades take effect immediately and you're billed a prorated amount for the remaining billing period. Downgrades and cancellations take effect at end of current period. You retain full access until then.
Is my data used to train Area models?
No. API data is never used to train models by default. Enterprise customers get zero data retention (ZDR) mode — no request or response data stored after processing. See our Privacy Policy and DPA for full details.
Do you offer startup or academic discounts?
Yes. Early-stage startups get $500 in free credits. Academic institutions and registered non-profits get 50% off Pro and Team plans. Contact us with proof of eligibility to apply for any of these programs.
What payment methods do you accept?
We accept all major credit and debit cards via Stripe. Enterprise customers can pay by invoice (Net-30 / Net-60), purchase orders, ACH transfer, or wire. Multi-year contracts with upfront payment discounts are also available.
